16 Design Principles

Energetic & Alive

Warm accents, pulsing indicators, and flowing animations make the UI feel active—like your channel.

Celebrate Wins

Milestones, confetti, and badges make growth feel rewarding. Your audience celebrates with you.

Content-First

Your videos are the star. UI supports your content with thumbnails, stats, and previews—never competing.

⚡

Real-Time Feedback

Live counters, floating hearts, and instant notifications keep you connected to your audience's reactions.

🎯

Visual Pipeline

See your content journey from idea to publish. Kanban boards and calendars with thumbnail previews—never just text.
